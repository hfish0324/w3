Fun Facts about Kodiak Bears

  1. Bears, specifically Kodiak Bears are my favorite animal.
  2. Kodiak bears are the largest bears in the world, with males weighing up to 1,500 pounds and standing over 10 feet tall on their hind legs.
  3. Despite their size, Kodiak bears are generally shy and elusive, often avoiding human contact.
  4. The oldest known wild Kodiak bear lived to be 34 years old.
